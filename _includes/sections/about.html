Learning and creating are where I thrive most, so I love that design allows me to live in the best of both worlds while providing the opportunity to explore infinite others. Outside of work, you can find me reading something I picked for its Goodreads reviews (aka the cover design), spiraling over my chess Elo, making music or chasing sunlight with my family and our gigantic cloud of floof, Dave, who is regularly accused, from rolled-down car windows, of being a horse, lion or bear.
